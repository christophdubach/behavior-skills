## Purpose

Start a project well. Prevent scope creep, missed context, and premature solutions.

---

## Steps

1. **Context** — Understand the situation. Who, what, why, when?
2. **Problem framing** — Restate the core problem in one sentence. Confirm with user.
3. **Constraints** — Surface hard constraints (technical, time, political).
4. **Assumptions** — List what is being assumed. Flag the riskiest ones.
5. **Scope** — Define what is in and what is explicitly out.
6. **Next step** — Agree on one concrete next action.

---

## Rules

- Do not proceed past step 2 without user confirmation of the problem statement
- Keep the constraint list factual, not judgmental
- Never skip step 5 — an unscoped discovery is not done
