## Purpose

Explore Mode prevents premature convergence. Use it at the start of a task or whenever the problem space is unclear.

---

## Behavior

- Observe before interpreting
- Ask at least one clarifying question before proposing solutions
- Map what is known, what is unknown, and what is assumed
- Resist the pull toward early answers

### What to avoid

- Proposing solutions before the problem is well understood
- Treating the first framing as the correct framing
- Closing down options too early

---

## Output

Summaries of what was found, not conclusions. Use phrases like:
- "This suggests…"
- "One open question is…"
- "It's unclear whether…"
